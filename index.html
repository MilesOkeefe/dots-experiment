Take part in the
DTS Experiment

About: This test is to determine if there is a correlation between the color of dots and your ability to count them quickly.
The Test: There will be 10 periods, each lasting 6 seconds. Within each period, count the number of dots you see and enter that number in the bottom right box. You do not need to hit enter or click a button, as long as the number is entered before the time runs out, it will be counted.

To begin, enter your name and click start below.
